Unix Archive Access Request Information

Access to the Unix Archive can now be obtained with no human intervention. First, read SCO's Ancient UNIX source license. If you agree with the terms of this license, then go to the Unix Archive Access Form.

Fill in your full name and e-mail address, and you will be given immediate access to the archive. You can then go to the list of Unix Archive mirrors.
