Printed Material for PDP Unix

Dennis Ritchie, one of the authors of Unix, keeps a lot of historical textual material on his web page. It makes interesting reading!

One important historical document, the 6th Edition Commentary by John Lions, is now available as a book.

See the Bibliography for a list of publications related to PDP Unix. Related to these publications are the Usenet News Postings about early Unix by various people.
