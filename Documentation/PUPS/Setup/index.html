UNIX Installation Instructions

Here are the manual pages for setup of some of the UNIX flavours in the archive. Note that the 6th and 7th Edition Unix manuals are owned by SCO, and are provided with their permission.

5th Edition Unix is similar to 6th Edition & so you can use the 6th Edition manuals. Both 5th and 6th Edition came as RK05 disk pack images. You will have lots of trouble installing either if you don't have RK05s.

  • 6th Edition installation instructions in HTML, PostScript and plain text format.
  • 7th Edition installation instructions in HTML, PostScript and plain text formats.
  • 7th Edition system regeneration instructions in HTML, PostScript and plain text formats.
  • 2.9BSD setup instructions in HTML, Pdf, PostScript and plain text formats.
  • 2.11BSD setup instructions in HTML, Pdf, PostScript and plain text formats.
