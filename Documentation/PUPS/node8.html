Programs Related to PDP Unix

  Programs related to these old versions of Unix fall into the following categories:

  • Application programs for early Unixes. The best place to look for these are in archives of the comp.sources.unix and comp.sources.misc Usenet archives.
  • Programs that can read/write filesystem and archive formats used in early Unixes.
  • Software emulators for various members of the PDP-11 family.
Many programs in the last two categories are available at minnie.cs.adfa.edu.au.
