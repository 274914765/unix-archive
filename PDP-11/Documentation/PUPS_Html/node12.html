Related FTP Sites

  • sunsite.unc.edu has some PDP-11 information via ftp.
  • The official patches to 2.11BSD are made available by Steven Schultz at ftp.iipo.gtegsc.com.

Related Usenet Newsgroups

There are no specific Usenet newsgroups about early Unix versions, but if you are after information about PDP-11 hardware and software, try alt.sys.pdp11 and vmsnet.pdp-11.

An FTP archive of comp.std.unix has some information about early Unix.

There is a small archive of usenet News for 1981-1982 on the web, too. The following groups contain articles relevant to early Unix: FA.unix-wizards, NET.bugs, NET.bugs.2bsd, NET.bugs.4bsd, NET.bugs.v7, NET.sources, NET.tools, NET.unix, and NET.unix-wizards.
