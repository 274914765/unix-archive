PUPS Archive Access Request Information

Access to the PUPS Archive can now be obtained with no human intervention. Go to SCO's free UNIX source license web page, and click to agree to the SCO license. On the next page, click on `apply for access to the PUPS Archive'. This will take you to a web form on the main PUPS Archive machine. Fill in your full name and e-mail address, and you will be given immediate access to the archive. You can then go to the list of PUPS Archive mirrors.
